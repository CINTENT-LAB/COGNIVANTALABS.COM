How the loop works

Ask, decide, act — then learn.

ASK

Every signal becomes an input.

Text, voice, documents, data feeds, sensors, vision and existing workflows all flow in alongside context: intent, situational context, objective, history, environment and the user themselves.

DECIDE

The CINTENT™ core reasons over it.

Intent, context and memory are weighed against constraints and governance policy to produce one thing: a decision — not another paragraph of prose.

ACT

A structured, governed output.

Example: "Approve · Path A" at 87% confidence, low risk, 4 constraints evaluated, routed for human approval — then executed into workflows across healthcare, finance, autonomy, robotics and infrastructure, with outcomes learned back into the loop.
