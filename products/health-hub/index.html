Capabilities

What Health Hub actually does.

Emergency triage support

Rapidly integrates available information and surfaces likely diagnoses and immediate care pathways without slowing workflow.

Drug interaction checking

Checks the patient's specific drug profile and shows interaction risks graded by severity, integrated with hospital formulary.

Allergy & contraindication validation

Automatically screens recommendations against allergies, contraindications, and genetic factors.

Patient-specific pathway adaptation

Customizes evidence-based guidelines to age, comorbidities, organ function, and prior treatment response.

Resource-aware recommendations

Suggests equally effective, currently-available care pathways when beds, staffing, or equipment are constrained.

Outcome tracking & learning

Tracks patient progress and improves recommendations for future, similar patient populations.

FAQ
Is it approved for clinical use?

The underlying MVP is in hospital pilot with FDA clearance in progress. It's designed to support clinician decision-making, not replace clinical judgment — all recommendations are reviewed and authorized by qualified clinical staff.

How does it handle HIPAA and patient privacy?

Designed to operate on-premise or on HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure. Patient data never leaves the hospital system; audit logging tracks all data access.

Can it integrate with existing EHR systems?

Built for HL7/FHIR interfaces with major EHR platforms (Epic, Cerner, Medidata). Integration typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on IT infrastructure.

Domain and functional scope confirmed at 4,500+ APIs — the live healthhub.cintent.tech domain isn't public yet, so treat specifics above as the MVP direction pending final sign-off.
